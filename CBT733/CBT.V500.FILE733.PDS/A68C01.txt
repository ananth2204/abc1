Note that square brackets are not defined for EBCDIC in System/370
Principles of Operation (GA22-7000).  The EBCDIC translation table
in the compiler accepts both hex 42 and AD for open square bracket,
and hex 43 and BD for close square bracket.

The following characters may also appear, but it is legitimate to
translate them to some dummy character, even blank.
