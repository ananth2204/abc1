TSO PARMLIB and CONSOLE commands

RACF

      SETROPTS CLASSACT(TSOAUTH)
      RDEFINE TSOAUTH PARMLIB
      RDEFINE TSOAUTH CONSOLE
      PERMIT PARMLIB CLASS(TSOAUTH) ID(STS01)
      PERMIT CONSOLE CLASS(TSOAUTH) ID(STS01)
      SETROPTS RACLIST(TSOAUTH)
