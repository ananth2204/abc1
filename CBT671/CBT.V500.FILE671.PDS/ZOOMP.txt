%EDITMAC  ------------------ZOOM    MACRO ----------------------------  TUTORIAL
%COMMAND ===>_ZCMD                                                             +
+
+The%ZOOM+macro will (1) EDIT a DSN (either sequential or PDS member)
+or; (2) BROWSE a DSN (sequential dataset or PDS member) when the optional
+character "B" is placed after the command (i.e. ZOOM B).  The ZOOM command
+can also be placed on a DDNAME concatenation and it will edit or browse each
+DSN in succession.
+
+USE:
+(A) Type%ZOOM+or %ZOOM B+on the COMMAND===> line and
+(B) Place the cursor on any dataset name  or DDNAME
+
%COMMAND ===> (1) ZOOM or (2) ZOOM B  or (3) add ZOOM to a PFKey
+//...............
+//....................
+//IN   DD    DISP=SHR,DSN=XXXXX.AAA  %<PLACE CURSOR ANYWHERE ON THE DDNAME>+
+//     DD    DISP=SHR,DSN=XXXXX.BBB  %<  OR ANYWHERE ON AN INDIVIDUAL DSN>+
+//...............
+//....................
)END
