
Commands entered in the area following "COMMAND=>" on the top line
are "primary" commands.  Primary commands are stored and are
"recallable".  To recall commands one at a time in reverse order,
enter a question mark (?) in the primary command area.

Once a command has been recalled, it can be over-typed if desired
and re-entered by pressing the ENTER key.

Up to the last 20 commands may be RECALLED.
