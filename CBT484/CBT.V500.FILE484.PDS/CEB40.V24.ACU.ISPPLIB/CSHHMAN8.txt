%COMMAND ===>_ZCMD                                                             +

   The%Selection+panel displays a list of controllers you have selected for
   inclusion in this selection group.  Selection groups are used to 'filter'
   which controllers will appear on the controller panel.

   On this panel you may enter a comment to describe each controller for
   future reference.  You may also update the other fields.  To save or
   replace a group, enter the member name you wish to use for this group and
   press the ENTER key.  If you choose not to save the member enter CANCEL
   in the command area of press the PF3 key.
