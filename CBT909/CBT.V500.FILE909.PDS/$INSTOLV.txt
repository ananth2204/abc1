If you want to email from Online Viewing you will need to do the following:

1. You will need make sure PTF RO69139 is applied in CA-Dispatch.

2. You will need to modify and apply DSINEX07 to turn on feature 59 in
   CA-Dispatch like this:
   FEAT059=Y, REPRINT WITH RECIPIENT DESCRIPTION
   0 RC expected. F LLA,REFRESH and recycle DISPATCH

3. Follow the steps in member OLVSETUP to setup a recipient for email from OLV
