Make XMITIP datasets SYSP.XMITIP.* or change names in MAILIT program.

Change in SYSP.XMITIP.EXEC(XMITIPCU):
       _center_ = "NKAISERA"
                to
       _center_ = "Your.site.com"

       STANDARD_TIME = "PST"
       DAYLIGHT_TIME = "PDT"
                to
       STANDARD_TIME = "EST"
       DAYLIGHT_TIME = "EDT"
                or whatever your timezone is.
