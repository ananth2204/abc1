 Name:        SCCO2D

 Purpose:     Convert a specified base 8 number to base 10.

 Environment: REXX subroutine/function for all MVS/ESA environments.
              SCCD2O resides in the IRXFLOC "function package".

 Arguments: 1 The (octal) number to be converted.

 Results:     The number expressed in base 10.
