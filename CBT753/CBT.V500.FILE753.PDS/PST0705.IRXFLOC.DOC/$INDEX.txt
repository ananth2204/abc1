    SCCPDSR    Read a specified member from a DDname.
    SCCPWR2    Calculate the next higher power of 2.
    SCCQWVAR   Extract a specified QwikRef item.
    SCXAMS     Issue IDCAMS command(s) and 'stem' the results.
    SCXSORT    Sort files and/or stem values.
    VARLIST    List all defined variables and their values.
    VOL4DD     Obtain the volume serial of a specified DDname.
    WILDCARD   Does a specified value match a specified pattern?
    XDATE      Date conversions.
