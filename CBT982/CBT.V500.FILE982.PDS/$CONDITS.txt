
 Costs and Contract

Mainframe Users:

For Hercules Users and those assisting making Z/OS easier to use,
or some way spreading knowledge of the platform, Jol is free.

For normal Commercial Use, the cost varies from $1.00 per user
for non-profits to $5.00 a day per user for normal commercial
enterprises.

Commercial Unlimited Site Licences start at $99,000.

Source Code is available for security and enhancements. It is
available on the clear understanding that it may not be used to
develop competing products.
