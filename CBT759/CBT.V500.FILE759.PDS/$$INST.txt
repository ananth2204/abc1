
Modify members ASMCAPX, ASMMAP, ASMPRNT and ASMTRAN according
to your JCL standards then submit them.

Member CAPXRDO contains RDO definitions for the programs, mapset
and TDQueues.

Member SITOVER contains SIT overrides for specifying TDQ names. If you
change them then the changes must be reflected in CAPXRDO.

Transaction MQTT allows you to specify which transactions you are
going to trace.
