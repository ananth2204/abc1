This utility is able to get a interactive report from a LOGREC
from MVS on-line file or archived, without to submit job jcl.

It was tested with OS/390 rel. 2.6 and as far as z/OS rel. 1.12

The EREP package is a mix of 1 Rexx pgm and 6 ISPF panels,
plus, not mandatory, a file/library for defaults list archive.
The output will get in view before to put on jes2/spool by
"PRINTDS" utility program.

I've package in ZIP file, but I add in a TXT file the same
library enclosed in XMIT format, just to look the contents.

You can find $$README member for instruction and
a "WORD" manual (in Italian language) for example.
