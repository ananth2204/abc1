NAccess Method Services for Catalogs
N-----------------------------------
NChapter 33. VERIFY
N------------------
NThe VERIFY command causes a catalog to correctly reflect the end of a
NVSAM data set after an error occurs while closing a VSAM data set. The
Nerror might have caused the catalog to be incorrect. When you add the
NRECOVER parameter, the VERIFY command also completes the interrupted
NVSAM processing.
