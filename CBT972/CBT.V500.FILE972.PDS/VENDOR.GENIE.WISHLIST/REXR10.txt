Nz/OS TSO/E REXX Reference
N=========================
NChapter 10. TSO/E REXX commands
N-------------------------------
NTSO/E provides TSO/E REXX commands to perform different services, such
Nas I/O and data stack requests. The TSO/E REXX commands are not the
Nsame as TSO/E command processors, such as ALLOCATE and PRINTDS. In
Ngeneral, you can only use these commands in REXX execs (in any address
Nspace), not in CLISTs or from TSO/E READY mode. The exceptions are the
NEXECUTIL command and the immediate commands HE, HI, HT, RT, TE, and TS.
