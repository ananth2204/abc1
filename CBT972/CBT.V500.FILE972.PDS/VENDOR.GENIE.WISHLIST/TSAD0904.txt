NReceiving master trace
N----------------------
NMaster trace writes trace data in the master trace table, which resides
Nin the master scheduler address space (ASID 1). You can obtain master
Ntrace data in a stand-alone, SVC, or unformatted dump, if the dump
Noptions list includes TRT to request trace data.
