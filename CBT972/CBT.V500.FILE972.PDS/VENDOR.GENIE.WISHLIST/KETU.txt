NIBM utilities and programs treated like utilities.

IMy sequence is based on the WISHLIST naming convention for
Iindividual WISH members. I had to dream up Chapter designations
Iusing alphabetic characters in order to avoid the future
Iaddition of a utility in numbered chapters and resequencing
Ithe existing WISH members.
