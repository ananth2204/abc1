NTools and Service Aids
N======================
NChapter 13: GETMAIN, FREEMAIN, STORAGE (GFS) trace
N--------------------------------------------------
NGFS trace is a diagnostic tool that collects information about the use
Nof the GETMAIN, FREEMAIN, or STORAGE macro. You can use GFS trace to
Nanalyze the allocation of virtual storage and identify users of large
Namounts of virtual storage.
N
NYou must use the generalized trace facility (GTF) to get the GFS trace
Ndata output.
* End of wish.
