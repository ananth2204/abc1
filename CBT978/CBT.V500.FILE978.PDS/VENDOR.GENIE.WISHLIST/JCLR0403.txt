NChapter 4. Syntax of Parameters
N-------------------------------
NSyntax Notes
N------------
NJCL positional parameters and keywords can have at most two levels of
Nsubparameters. Therefore, when parentheses are used to delimit a list
Nof subparameters, a maximum of two levels of parenthesis nesting is
Npermitted. This restriction applies even if the parentheses are empty.
