NChapter 10. TSO/E REXX commands
N-------------------------------
NTE
N--
NTE (Trace End) is an immediate command you can use to end tracing REXX
Nexecs. The TE immediate command is available if an exec is running in
NTSO/E and you press the attention interrupt key to enter attention
Nmode. You can enter TE in response to the REXX attention prompting
Nmessage, IRX0920I. The exec continues processing, but tracing is off.
N
NSyntax
D  TE
