NFUZZ
N----
Nreturns the current setting of NUMERIC FUZZ. See the NUMERIC
Ninstruction for more information.
N
N
NSyntax:
D  FUZZ()
N
N
NExample
N-------
I  current_fuzz = FUZZ()
I  SAY "The current FUZZ is" current_fuzz"."
