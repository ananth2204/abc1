NChapter 8. PSF Parameters
N-------------------------
NPRINTDEV statement
N------------------
NThe PRINTDEV statement specifies parameters for printer initialization.
NIn deferred-printing mode, PRINTDEV is among the JCL statements in the
NPSF startup procedure. In direct-printing mode, PRINTDEV is among the
NJCL statements associated with the application program.
