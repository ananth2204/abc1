NChapter 6. Numbers and arithmetic
N---------------------------------
NDefinition
N----------
NErrors
N------
NTwo types of errors may occur during arithmetic:
N
NOverflow or Underflow
N---------------------
NBecause this allows for (very) large exponents, overflow or underflow
Nis treated as a syntax error.
N
N
NInsufficient storage
N--------------------
NStorage is needed for calculations and intermediate results, and on
Noccasion an arithmetic operation may fail because of lack of storage.
NThis is considered a terminating error as usual, rather than an
Narithmetic error.
