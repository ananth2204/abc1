NEntering Jobs
N-------------
NJob Steps
N---------
NYou enter a program into the operating system as a job step. A job
Nstep consists of the job control statements that request and control
Nexecution of a program and request the resources needed to run the
Nprogram. A job step is identified by an EXEC statement. The job step
Ncan also contain data needed by the program. The operating system
Ndistinguishes job control statements from data by the contents of
Nthe records.
N
NJobs
N----
NA job is a collection of related job steps. A job is identified by
Na JOB statement.
