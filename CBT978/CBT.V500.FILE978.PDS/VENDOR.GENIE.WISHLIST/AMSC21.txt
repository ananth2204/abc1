NChapter 21. DIAGNOSE

NThe DIAGNOSE command scans a basic catalog structure (BCS) or a VSAM
Nvolume data set (VVDS) to validate the data structures and detect
Nstructure errors.
N
NSearch Chapter 22, "EXAMINE" for information on the EXAMINE command,
Nwhich can inspect the structural integrity of the data or index
Ncomponent of a key-sequenced data set cluster or of a BCS.
