NExpressions
N-----------
NExpressions consist of one or more terms (literal strings, symbols,
Nfunction calls, or subexpressions) interspersed with zero or more
Noperators that denote operations to be carried out on terms. A
Nsubexpression is a term in an expression bracketed within a left and
Na right parenthesis.
