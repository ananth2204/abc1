N-----------------------------------
NChapter 16. DEFINE NONVSAM
N--------------------------
NThe DEFINE NONVSAM command defines a catalog entry for non-VSAM data
Nsets or a collection of objects.
N
NDEFINE NONVSAM does not allocate space on the volume. To allocate the
Nprimary space on a volume for a new NONVSAM data set, create the data
Nset.
