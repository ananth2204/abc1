NChapter 2. Job Control Tasks

NProcessing Jobs
N---------------
NThe operating system performs many job control tasks automatically.
NYou can influence the way your job is processed by the JCL and JES2
Nor JES3 parameters you code. For example, the job entry subsystem
Nselects jobs for execution, but you can speed up or delay selection
Nof your job by the parameters you code.
