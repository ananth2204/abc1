NJES2 Control Statement
N======================
NINTRDR /*DEL Statement
N----------------------
NThis control statement cancels the job, and requests the output from
Nthe job. The job is immediately scheduled for output processing. The
Noutput will consist of any JCL submitted so far, followed by a
Nmessage indicating that the job has been deleted before execution.
