NCharacters
N----------
NA character is a member of a defined set of elements that is used for
Nthe control or representation of data. You can usually enter a
Ncharacter with a single keystroke.
M
MAvoid turning HEX ON to enter a character. Instead, define a variable
Mand assign the hexadecimal literal, as in cent_sign = '4A'x.
