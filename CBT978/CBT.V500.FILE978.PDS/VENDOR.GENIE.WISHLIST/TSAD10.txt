NChapter 10: The Generalized Trace Facility (GTF)
N------------------------------------------------
NThe generalized trace facility (GTF) is a service aid you can use to
Nrecord and diagnose system and program problems. GTF is part of the MVS
Nsystem product, and you must explicitly activate it by entering a
DSTART GTF.gtfname
Ncommand.
