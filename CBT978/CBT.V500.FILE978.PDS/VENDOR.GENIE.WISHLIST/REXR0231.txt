N  Assignments and symbols
N  -----------------------
N    Constant symbols
N    ----------------
NA constant symbol starts with a digit (0-9) or a period.
N
NYou cannot change the value of a constant symbol. It is simply the
Nstring consisting of the characters of the symbol (that is, with any
Nlowercase alphabetic characters translated to uppercase).
N
NExamples:
N---------
NThese are constant symbols:
N
D77
D827.53
D.12345
D12e5 /* Same as 12E5 */
D3D
D17E-3
